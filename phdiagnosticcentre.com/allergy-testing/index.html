ALLERGY TESTING

Allergies occur when your immune system reacts to certain foreign substances like dust, smoke, pollen that dont affect most of the people.

Allergy testing identifies allergens, like mold, pet dander, bee stings and peanuts, that can cause allergies. During the test, a healthcare provider introduces allergens into your skin to check for a rash or other reactions. Blood tests can check for IgE antibodies. The results can help you take steps to manage allergies.

The immune system response to allergy ranges from mild rashes to abdominal pain to a life-threatening complication called anaphylactic shock.

Symptoms of Allergies

  • Runny/Stuffy Nose
  • Itchy, red or watery eyes
  • Itchy Nose and throat
  • Coughing & Sneezing
  • Difficulty in breathing
  • Chest Tightness /pain
  • Wheezing
  • Trouble in sleeping
  • Headache
  • Fatigue
  • Tingling or itching in mouth
  • Hives
  • Failure to thrive
  • Skin Rashes
  • G/I symptoms
